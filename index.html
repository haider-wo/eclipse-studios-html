About Eclipse Studios

A team of visual storytellers pushing the boundaries of cinema

Founded in 2018, Eclipse Studios has grown into a collective of directors, VFX artists, cinematographers, and motion designers united by one mission: creating visuals that leave lasting impressions.

From blockbuster film VFX to brand-defining motion campaigns, we blend technical mastery with artistic vision to deliver work that stands out in an increasingly crowded visual landscape.
