Our Story

From a small studio to an award-winning collective

Founded in 2018 by a group of passionate filmmakers, Eclipse Studios began with a simple vision: to push the boundaries of visual storytelling. What started as a three-person team working out of a small Los Angeles studio has grown into a 45-member collective serving clients worldwide.

Over the past decade, we've had the privilege of working on over 250 projects — from indie films that premiered at Sundance to global brand campaigns reaching millions. Our work has been recognized with 32 industry awards, but what matters most to us is the impact our visuals have on audiences.

Today, we continue to blend technical mastery with artistic vision, delivering work that stands out in an increasingly crowded visual landscape.
