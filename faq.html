Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about working with Eclipse Studios.

Home/FAQ
We offer a comprehensive range of cinematic production services including film production, VFX & CGI, motion design, color grading, sound design, and brand identity. Each service is tailored to meet the specific needs of your project.
Our main studio is located in Los Angeles, California. We also have satellite studios in London and Tokyo, allowing us to serve clients globally with local expertise.
We work on a wide variety of projects including feature films, documentaries, commercials, music videos, brand campaigns, and digital content. Our team has experience across all genres and formats.
Project costs vary widely based on scope, complexity, and timeline. Our projects typically range from $10k for smaller productions to $500k+ for major feature films. We provide detailed quotes after understanding your specific needs.
Yes, we offer flexible payment plans for most projects. Typically, we structure payments in milestones: 30% upfront, 40% at mid-production, and 30% on delivery. We're happy to discuss options that work for your budget.
Timelines vary by project scope. A commercial might take 4-6 weeks, while a feature film can take 6-12 months. We'll provide a detailed timeline during our initial consultation based on your specific requirements.
Simply reach out through our contact form or email us directly. We'll schedule an initial consultation to discuss your vision, goals, and requirements. From there, we'll provide a detailed proposal and timeline.
Absolutely! We work with clients worldwide. Our global team and remote collaboration tools allow us to deliver exceptional results regardless of location. We've successfully completed projects across 4 continents.
We use industry-standard tools including Maya, Houdini, Nuke, After Effects, DaVinci Resolve, and Unreal Engine. Our team is proficient in all major VFX, animation, and post-production software.
We include unlimited revisions as part of our standard package. We believe in iterating until the work exceeds your expectations. Our goal is your complete satisfaction with the final deliverable.
